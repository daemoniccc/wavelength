Wavelength

Wavelength is a guessing game where one player gives a clue, and everyone else tries to place a marker on a hidden spectrum between two extremes.

How to Play

  • One player is randomly chosen as the Psychic.
  • The Psychic sees a category and two opposite ends of a spectrum.
  • The game secretly chooses a target position on that spectrum.
  • The Psychic gives one hint that fits the category.
  • Everyone else places their arrow where they think the target is.
  • The closer you are, the better you did.

Choose a Mode

Normal mode is safe, casual, and suitable for pretty much anyone. Not Normal mode was made by Grok AI have fun!
